About Us

History

ERamen's story began in 1910 in a small town in Japan when a passionate ramen enthusiast named Hiroshi Tanaka opened a humble noodle stall. Hiroshi was dedicated to perfecting the art of ramen-making, and his flavorful creations quickly gained popularity among locals and travelers alike. Over the years, his ramen shop became renowned for its authentic flavors and exceptional quality, earning it a reputation as one of the oldest and most beloved ramen establishments in the world.

In 2015, inspired by the global demand for authentic Japanese cuisine, Hiroshi's descendants expanded the legacy of ERamen beyond Japan and opened their first international branch in Indonesia. With a desire to share their family's century-old tradition and passion for ramen, ERamen quickly became a favorite among Indonesian food enthusiasts, continuing the rich heritage of the brand in a new cultural setting.

Vision

ERamen's vision is to be recognized as the global ambassador of authentic ramen, providing a truly immersive and delightful dining experience for customers around the world. Drawing from their rich history and expertise, ERamen aims to capture the essence of traditional Japanese ramen and introduce it to diverse cultures, promoting an appreciation for the artistry and flavors of this iconic dish.



Mission

1. Quality and Organic Ingredients: ERamen is committed to using the finest, organic ingredients to create their ramen noodles and other menu items. By prioritizing quality and sustainability, they strive to offer healthier options to customers without compromising on taste.
2. Authentic Flavors: ERamen aims to preserve the authenticity of traditional ramen recipes, carefully honed over generations. They meticulously craft each bowl of ramen to ensure it reflects the original flavors and techniques that have made ERamen a culinary institution.
3. Culinary Innovation: While staying true to tradition, ERamen also embraces culinary innovation. They constantly explore new flavors, combinations, and presentations to offer a diverse range of ramen options that cater to different tastes and preferences. This allows them to continuously surprise and delight their customers.
