ERamen Menu

Tonkotsu Ramen

Tonkotsu ramen is a popular Japanese dish known for its rich and creamy broth. It is made by simmering pork bones for hours, resulting in a flavorful and collagen-rich soup. The noodles are typically thin and straight, and the dish is often topped with sliced pork, green onions, seaweed, and other garnishes.

Cost : Rp. 25.000

Soyu Ramen

Soyu ramen, also referred to as shoyu ramen, is a classic Japanese ramen variety that features a soy sauce-based broth. The broth is typically clear and savory, with a delicate balance of salty and umami flavors. Along with the soy sauce, it may include other seasonings like mirin, dashi, and ginger. Common toppings include roasted pork, bamboo shoots, nori, and green onions.

Cost : Rp. 50.000

Fukuoka Ramen

Fukuoka ramen is a style of ramen originating from Fukuoka, a city in southern Japan. It is characterized by its rich, pork-based tonkotsu broth, which is often paired with thin and straight noodles. Fukuoka ramen commonly features toppings such as sliced pork, spicy pickled ginger, green onions, and may include a variety of additional ingredients like bean sprouts or kikurage mushrooms.

Cost : Rp. 35.000

Miso Ramen

Miso ramen is a type of Japanese ramen that features a savory and hearty broth flavored with fermented soybean paste called miso. The broth has a deep umami taste and is often complemented with toppings such as sliced pork, bean sprouts, corn, and green onions. Miso ramen is known for its robust and slightly sweet flavor profile.

Cost : Rp. 40.000

Buldak Ramen

Buldak ramen, also known as "fire chicken ramen," is a popular Korean instant noodle dish that is famously spicy. The noodles are cooked in a fiery chili-based sauce, creating a tongue-tingling experience. It is often enjoyed with additional toppings like sliced cheese, boiled eggs, and scallions to balance out the heat.

Cost : Rp. 55.000

Chicken Ramen

Chicken ramen refers to ramen dishes that primarily use chicken as the main ingredient in their broth. The broth is typically lighter compared to pork-based ramen and has a clean and subtle flavor. The noodles can vary in thickness and shape, and the dish is often garnished with sliced chicken, green onions, nori, and other complementary toppings. Chicken ramen offers a milder alternative to other ramen varieties.

Cost : Rp. 20.000
